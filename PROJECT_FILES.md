"Crowdfunding Description"

This project analyzes the global crowdfunding ecosystem by integrating multiple Business Intelligence tools — Excel, SQL, Tableau, and Power BI. The goal was to transform raw crowdfunding data into actionable insights that reveal funding drivers, backer behaviors, and campaign success factors.

Key outcomes include:

- Comprehensive KPIs: Success rates, funding volumes, campaign durations, and category-level performance.
- Interactive Dashboards: Visualizations in Tableau and Power BI with slicers, filters, and benchmarks for strategic decision-making.
- Data Integration: SQL queries structured the analysis, ensuring consistency across platforms.
- Insights Delivered:
- Peak funding months and regional hotspots.
- Backer segmentation by category and geography.
- Creator benchmarking and campaign flow analysis.
  
By combining multiple BI tools, this project demonstrates how data storytelling and visualization can support business strategy, highlight opportunities, and showcase technical expertise in analytics.
